Our Success

Happy Families

Parents and referring professionals routinely thank us for making this challenging event such a manageable, safe, and therapeutic experience. That’s because we:

  • Reduce Anxiety: We take the fear and mystery out of the experience for the parents their adolescent or young adult
  • Communicate and Accommodate: We work closely with parents, the receiving program, and referring professionals to ensure that travel arrangements and staff selection are ideal and special needs are accommodated
  • Manage Expenses: We work hard on our clients’ behalf to manage expenses and provide the quickest and safest transportation at the lowest cost

Believe it or not even the most resistant teens often thank and even hug our staff at the end of the transport experience! By the time they arrive at treatment, many have come to understand their need for treatment are relieved that the process is finally underway.

If you are a parent or professional interested in our services and would like to speak with a parent or referent, please let us know.
